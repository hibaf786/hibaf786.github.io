Background on Asia

To many Asia just may be another continent, but it's where my home resides. Being born in Lahore, Pakistan, I have spent my entire life there until I moved to the US for my Undergraduate degree.

Land Boundaries

Asia is known for being the largest continent in the world. It is surrounded by the Indian, Pacific, and Arctic Oceans. It also shares a boundary with Europe.

Here are some fun facts about nature in Asia:
