Memorable Dining Experience

The most memorable dining experience I have, as biased as it may sound, would most likely have to be in my home city. There is a restaurant called Haveli, located in the older part of the city. It's a rooftop restaurant that lets you oversee the historic aspects of the city. Not only is the food amazing, but the environment and ambience is just as good.

Most Memorable Adventure

The most thrilling activity I've ever done is sky diving at Key West, FL. It was a surreal experience, and one that I would recommend to everyone. The seven seconds spent in just free fall was an experience I still cannot find the words to describe.
