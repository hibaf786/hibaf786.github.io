My First Adventure

I was about seven years old when I travelled for the very first time. My family and I went to Thailand, where we spent most of our time on their island Phuket. Being on the island allowed me to have my first experiences with water activities such as parasailing, riding the banana boat, and seaTREK diving. I visited a waterfall for the very first time, and had a great time exploring Phuket and experiencing Thai culture.
