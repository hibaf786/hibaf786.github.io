Most Memorable Adventure

When looking back, my time in Venice was a very unique experience. Venice was a city like no other, beautiful in every way. Instead of having car vehicles as taxis, they used boats as taxis. In that itself the city was unique. I had the chance to take a gondola ride in Venice, and it was a great time. Although the gondola was rocky and there was more than one instance where the gondola almost turned over, it was still a great time.
