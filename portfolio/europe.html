Memorable Dining Experience

While on a school trip to Paris, I had the opportunity to try out the French Cuisine. For the first time ever, I had snails, and it was quite an experience. Although the snails had a very chewy touch to it that gave it a yummy touch, the idea of eating snails was very hard.

My First Adventure

I was about seven years old when I traveled for the very first time. My family and I went to Thailand, where we spent most of our time on the island of Phuket. Being on the island allowed me to have my first experiences with water activities such as parasailing, riding the banana boat, and seaTREK diving. I visited a waterfall for the very first time, and had a great time exploring Phuket and experiencing Thai culture.
