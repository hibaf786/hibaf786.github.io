Introduction

Spain went from being a power full empire in the 16th and 17th centuries to falling behind Britain, France, and Germany. Spain saw economic growth after the death of dictator Francisco Franco. Spain went through a major economic recession in 2008, but the country is now the largest economy in Europe.

Environment - current issues

Spain suffers from pollution caused by the Mediterranean Sea. The Mediterranean Sea suffers from raw sewage, oil, and gas. Spain also suffers from environment issues such as air pollution, deforestation, and desertification.

Independence

Spain gained its independence in 1492 when small Christian redoubts of the north began to conquer and unite several kingdoms that were previously under Muslim occupation.

National Holiday

12 October is known as Spain's National Day.

This date comes from when the Spanish gained independence Oct. 12, 1492.
